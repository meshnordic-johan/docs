## The basics

Mesh Nordic AI is an intelligent personalization engine that provides powerful Composable Commerce components for your e-commerce website. It allows you to display the right product at the right time to the right target audience.

We offer Composable Commerce components for:

- AI Search
- AI Recommendations

In only a couple of hours, you can integrate all of these surfaces and uplift any metric you want to focus on.

To learn more about our product, check out our [website](https://www.meshnordic.ai).


<br/>
## Quick start

To integrate with Mesh Nordic AI and ingest your data into our systems, you need to either:

1. Choose the integration guide that fits your needs, or
2. Follow the product feeds guide, or
3. Use the Headless API integration for custom sites.
